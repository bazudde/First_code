Bliss

I see the orange and yellow
Reminding me I've made it another day
I see the green
I see the brown
Together showing me my heritage
I see the blue turn black
As the waters came down
Cleansing all that is stained
Finally,I see happiness,
My favorite color
When you smile at me.
